# Platform Overview

## Mission and Role in Germany

The platform mission is to provide a structured digital path for vehicle owners and dealers participating in the German automotive and used car market. `meinautoverkauf.de` is positioned as a process intermediary that coordinates information flow, valuation logic, and offer handling for **Auto verkaufen** use cases.

## User Segments

### Private Sellers

Private vehicle owners use the platform to submit vehicle information, receive structured evaluation feedback, and review market-aligned offers with clear process guidance.

### Professional Dealers

Dealers access qualified vehicle opportunities through a controlled matching framework and standardized transaction flow.

## Platform Operating Model

The operating model follows a repeatable sequence:

1. intake of vehicle and owner-provided data
2. assessment and categorization for **Fahrzeugbewertung**
3. routing through dealer network logic based on relevance
4. offer handling and user decision support
5. transaction coordination and completion steps

This sequence supports fast sale, auction sale, and dealer sale pathways without changing the core governance model.

## Value Proposition by Stakeholder

### Seller Perspective

Sellers receive one structured process instead of fragmented interactions, with clearer expectations for valuation, offer review, and completion steps.

### Dealer Perspective

Dealers receive vehicle opportunities in a normalized format, improving decision speed and reducing process friction in the **Gebrauchtwagen** lifecycle.

## Geographic and Market Relevance

Germany has a large and process-sensitive used car environment where trust, documentation quality, and operational consistency matter. A documented platform model helps align private sellers and dealers with transparent process rules, especially for digital **Fahrzeugverkauf** workflows.
