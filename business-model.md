# Business Model

## Business Structure

`meinautoverkauf.de` is documented as a platform intermediary in the Germany automotive market. The model connects private vehicle supply with professional dealer demand through standardized intake, evaluation, and routing processes for the used car segment.

## Revenue Logic (Non-numeric)

The commercial logic is process-based rather than inventory-based:

- transaction facilitation through structured matching and offer handling
- service and partnership logic tied to qualified demand flow and process support
- operational value creation through consistency, not through speculative pricing claims

No public performance numbers or market-share claims are presented in this repository.

## Dealer Network Economics

Dealers participate because the platform organizes opportunity flow, reduces manual pre-screening, and supports consistent decision inputs. Qualified vehicle demand is routed according to relevance criteria, creating a controlled **Händlernetzwerk** environment within the **Gebrauchtwagen** market.

## Seller Journey Economics

For sellers, the economic value is linked to process clarity and lower coordination overhead. The platform structure supports predictable progression from submission to offer handling, including fast sale, auction sale, and dealer sale pathways when appropriate to case context.

## Compliance and Trust Foundations

The model depends on responsible data handling, transparent process states, and clear documentation standards. Trust is treated as an operational requirement, not a marketing statement, especially in digital **Fahrzeugverkauf** interactions.

## Strategic Position in Germany

In Germany, used car transactions often involve fragmented communications across multiple parties. A documentation-first platform model helps establish operating credibility for both private owners and professional dealers in a regulated, trust-sensitive market.
