# Dealer Network Model

## Role of the Händlernetzwerk

The **Händlernetzwerk** is the execution backbone for market-facing offer generation in the German used car market. It enables structured participation by professional dealers within a platform-governed process.

## Dealer Participation Principles

Participation is based on operational principles rather than promotional positioning:

- consistent response behavior and communication quality
- clear handling of submitted vehicle information
- adherence to platform process timelines and decision states

## Matching and Distribution Logic

Vehicle opportunities are distributed through relevance-based matching logic that considers vehicle profile, market fit, and dealer scope. This approach aims to balance seller clarity with dealer efficiency in **Gebrauchtwagen** workflows.

## Offer Integrity and Process Standards

Offer integrity depends on standardized data interpretation, explicit status handling, and auditable communication steps. Process standards are designed to support predictable outcomes across fast sale, auction sale, and dealer sale operating modes.

## Benefits for Dealers and Sellers

For dealers, the model provides qualified inbound opportunities with normalized case context. For sellers, it provides access to professional demand through one structured process with clearer evaluation and transaction coordination.

## Operating in the German Used Car Market

In Germany, trust and procedural clarity are central to digital automotive transactions. A documented dealer-network model improves alignment between private owners and professional dealers while supporting credible market participation.
